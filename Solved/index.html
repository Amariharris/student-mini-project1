Welcome To CloudLand Canyon

The state park contains over 7 main trails, and an abundance of over fun things for all ages! Our park contains camp sites, cottages, A Yurt vilage, Rest Station, Parking, and its very own Interpretive Center!


TRAILS

Sitton's Gulch Trail

  • Trail totals 6 miles roundtrip difficulty: Strenuous
  • Beginning at the Main Trailhead, Sitton follows the Waterfalls Trail down into the canyon, then continues all the way down to the canyon mouth in trenton.

Bear Creek Trail

  • 7 mile roundtrip difficulty: moderate
  • The creek can be ankle- to waist-deep deending on rainfall and can be difficult as there is no bridge or dry crossing available. After crossing the creek, the trail continues up until it becomes a loop around the northeastern portion of the park.

Overlook Trail

  • 1 mile roundtrip difficulty: Easy
  • Offers probably the best views in the park, the trail meanders along the rim of CloudLand Canyon for approximately half a mile.
